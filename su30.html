Sukhoi Su-30

Overview

The Sukhoi Su-30 is a twin-engine, multirole fighter developed by Russia's Sukhoi Aviation Corporation. Known for its versatility and advanced avionics, the Su-30 is capable of performing air superiority, ground attack, and reconnaissance missions. It has been widely exported and is in service with numerous air forces around the world, offering impressive maneuverability, advanced radar systems, and a range of weapons suitable for both air-to-air and air-to-ground combat.

Crash History

The Su-30 has generally shown a strong safety record, but there have been notable incidents and accidents due to mechanical failures, pilot errors, and operational hazards. Despite these, the Su-30 remains a reliable aircraft in service with multiple air forces worldwide, with ongoing improvements to avionics and structural components enhancing its safety and performance.
