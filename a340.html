Airbus A340-941

Overview

The Airbus A340-941 is a long-range, wide-body, four-engine commercial aircraft developed by Airbus. Part of the A340 family, the A340-900 is one of the largest variants, with a capacity to carry up to 375 passengers. It is designed for long-haul flights and is known for its excellent performance, comfort, and reliability. The A340-900 offers a range of up to 14,800 km (9,200 miles), making it suitable for intercontinental travel.

Crash History

The Airbus A340-941 has an excellent safety record with no major crashes directly attributable to its design. As with all aircraft, incidents involving technical issues or human error have occurred, but the A340-900 is equipped with the latest safety systems. The aircraft’s four engines provide redundancy and reliability, ensuring safety for long-haul flights. No significant issues have been recorded with the A340-900, making it one of the most reliable long-range aircraft.
