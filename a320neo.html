Airbus A320neo

Overview

The Airbus A320neo is a narrow-body, single-aisle aircraft that is part of the A320 family of airliners. It is the upgraded version of the original A320 and features new engines, improved aerodynamics, and increased fuel efficiency. The A320neo is popular with both low-cost carriers and major airlines around the world.

Crash History

The Airbus A320neo is a relatively new aircraft, and as of now, it has a solid safety record. However, as with all aircraft, there have been incidents reported. Notably, the A320neo is equipped with the latest safety features and has been certified by the aviation authorities. There have been no major crashes directly attributable to the A320neo itself, but incidents involving the older A320 family members and similar aircraft have led to improvements.
